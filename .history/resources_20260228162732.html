📚 Resources & Guides

Curated tips and exercises to support your mental wellness journey

🎯 Building Sustainable Habits

1

Start Small

Begin with one tiny habit. The goal is to make it small that it's easy to do every day.

2

Add new Habits

Attach new habits to existing routines. For example, after brushing your teeth, do 5 minutes of stretching.

3

Track Progress

Celebrate small wins. Consistency matters more than perfection.

4

Stay Accountable

Use MindTrace to visualize your progress and maintain motivation.

🧘 Reflection Exercises

✍️

Daily Gratitude

Spend 2 minutes writing three things you're grateful for today.

🧘

5-Minute Meditation

Find a quiet place and focus on your breathing. Apps like Headspace can guide you.

🚶

Mindful Walk

Take a 10-15 minute walk and notice your surroundings without distractions.

📓

Mood Reflection

At the end of day, reflect on your mood and what influenced it.
