About MindTrace

Building awareness through small habits and daily reflection

🎯 Our Mission

MindTrace is designed to help users build self-awareness through small, consistent habits and daily mood reflection. We believe that mental wellness is a journey, not a destination, and it starts with understanding yourself.

💡

🔄

Consistency

Small, consistent habits fit naturally into everyday routines without overwhelming users.

🌱

Growth

Track progress and celebrate small wins as you build your mental wellness journey.

🌟 What Makes MindTrace Different?

Unlike traditional mental health apps that focus on diagnosis or tracking symptoms, MindTrace emphasizes self-discovery and awareness. We combine mood tracking with habit building to help you understand the connection between what you do and how you feel.

Our visual approach makes it enjoyable to check in with yourself daily, building a deeper understanding of your emotional patterns and what influences them.
